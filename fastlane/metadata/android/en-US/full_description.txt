Amply provides quick access to supported Android battery charge-protection modes. On validated devices it can temporarily allow one full charge and restore the protective limit automatically. Direct control requires user-authorized WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS or Shizuku access.

Even without that setup, Amply is useful on any device:

- Charge alarm: pick a level and Amply reminds you to unplug when charging reaches it, so you can protect your battery manually.
- Battery details: a live view of level, temperature, voltage, current, charge cycles, and health.
- Protection guide: on devices Amply can't control directly, it points you to your phone's own built-in battery-protection setting.

These extra features need no ADB or Shizuku, and no special permissions beyond notifications for the alarm.
